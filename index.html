about us

M

vision and Mission

Our Vision: To become a centre of Academic Excellence founded on Biblical Christian Values Our Mission: Education in biblical perspective Our Objectives. Our primary objective is to establish a secure and efficient system for managing laptops within the library premises. By implementing strict identification requirements, advanced security measures, and comprehensive staff training, we aim to prevent theft and ensure the safety of borrowed laptops. Additionally, our goal is to streamline the borrowing process for patrons while maintaining accurate inventory records and promptly addressing any incidents. Through continuous monitoring, enforcement of policies, and regular improvements to our security protocols, we strive to create a trusted environment where patrons can confidently utilize library resources.

R

Rules and regulations

Patrons must provide identification to borrow laptops. Use security cameras and anti-theft devices. Maintain an inventory and conduct audits. Train staff on security protocols. Display borrowing policies prominently. Restrict access to laptop storage areas. Monitor usage and encourage reporting of incidents. Enforce penalties for theft. Regularly review and improve security measures.
